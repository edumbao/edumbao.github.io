My working process

I do not treat SEO content as random blog publishing. I build it as a system.

1. Start with real demand. Use Google Search Console, customer questions, ICP pain points, and product use cases to choose content topics.
2. Turn questions into structure. Build articles around direct answers, useful H2s, practical examples, and internal link paths.
3. Write for clarity and conversion. Make the page easy to understand, beginner-friendly, and connected to the next useful action.
4. Improve with repeatable workflows. Use templates, checklists, and review systems so the team can move faster without lowering quality.
